SERVICES

Long Arm quilting eliminates the challenges and time associated with finishing quilts on your home machine. It is a fun, efficient and ergonomically friendly quilting method that will add a whole new dimension to your quilt. At Sew Many Stitches, we are happy to offer Long Arm certification classes, rental and quilting services.

We offer edge to edge and custom quilting services, Long Arm certification classes and rental.

Types of machines: Lucey, Millennium, George

We have 3 APQS machines (American Professional Quilting Systems) for you to work with. Lucey and Millie both have a 26" throat giving you lots of quilting space, they are incredibly smooth to operate. Additionally, Millie has a computerized system. George is a sit-down machine with a 20" throat on a 6' table.

Certification & Cost (Long Arm):

Certification consists of 3 hours of in class instruction and 3 hours of post-certification rental time to work on your own project. Certification costs $150.00. Certification must be completed in order to purchase rental time on our Long Arm machines.

About Sew Many Stitches

Sew Many Stitches is a Newfoundland owned and operated, full service quilt shop. It started out as a labour of love in the basement of owner Debbie Northover's home in 2008. An avid quilter, Debbie desired finishing her quilts with ease and comfort using a Long Arm quilting machine, from there a business was born. Debbie became a dealer for APQS and Brother, selling machines, custom quilts, and offering quilting services to customers; eventually teaching classes in her home.
